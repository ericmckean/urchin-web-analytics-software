My shopping cart has different a domain name than my store site. How do I set up e-commerce tracking?

  • This article authored by ivantage Limited, an Urchin Software Authorized Consultant

Urchin can track data from a shopping trolley on your own domain or on other domains with the addition of some code. To accomplish this please read Tracking Secure Store Transactions. To set up your pages to report transaction data to Urchin, please read How do I track e-commerce transactions?
