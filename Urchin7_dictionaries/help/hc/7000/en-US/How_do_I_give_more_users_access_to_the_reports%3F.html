How do I give more users access to the reports?

Each user has his or her own password, regional setting, and level of access to reports.

How to Add Users

  1. Log in to Urchin 6.
  2. Click Configuration (at upper right of the interface).

  3. Click Users & Groups, then click Users

  4. Click Add. The User Wizard appears.
  5. Select a username (lower-case and without spaces) and choose a password.
  6. Enter the user's real name - this will be displayed in the Urchin system. Click Next.
  7. Select the Admin level. Click Finish.
  8. Select a Region for the new user.
  9. Click Finish.
