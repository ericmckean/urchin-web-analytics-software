How do I track clicks on outbound links?

If you have outbound links on your site, there is an easy way for you to track which links visitors click on to leave your site.

To track outbound clicks, add the following code within the <a> tag: 

    <a href="http://www.site.com" onClick="javascript:urchinTracker('/outboundlinks/sitename/pagename');">

This code causes each click on the outbound link to be logged as though it were a pageview named /outboundlinks/sitename/pagename. It is a good idea to log all of your outbound links into a logical directory structure as shown in the example. This way, you will be able to easily identify what pages visitors clicked on to leave your site.
