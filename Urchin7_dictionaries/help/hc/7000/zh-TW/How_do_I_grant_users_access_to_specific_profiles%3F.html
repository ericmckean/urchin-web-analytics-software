How do I grant users access to specific profiles?

To edit your Users's access to profiles:

  1. Log in to Urchin 6.
  2. Click Configuration, then Users.
  3. Click Edit next to the desired user.
  4. Click the Report Access tab. The available Profiles will be shown in the box at left.
  5. Select one or more Profiles. To select multiple Profiles, hold down the command or control key while selecting.
  6. Click the right-facing arrow to move the Profile(s) to the Access Granted box.
  7. Click Update to save changes.
