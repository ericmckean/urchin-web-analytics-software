Referral Errors

How can you track which sites have old or broken links to you? The Referral Errors report shows the errors reported in your logs and shows the number of errored request attempts. By clicking on the you can drilldown to the requested content on your site, and again to referral site, or the web page they came from before encountering the error on your site.

Click the button at the far left of any entry to view data over time.

To compare two date ranges, select the "Compare to Past" option on the Calendar tab.
