Country Drilldown

Where do my best customers live? This report aggregates revenue by geographic region.

Click the plus next to any entry to drill down and compare locations within a region.
Click the button at the far left of any entry to view data over time.
To compare two date ranges, select the "Compare to Past" option on the Calendar tab.

Revenue is the total revenue resulting from e-commerce transactions by visitors referred by the source. Trans is the number of transactions. $/Trans is Revenue divided by Transactions. Percent $ is the percentage of your site's total e-commerce revenue that visitors from a region contribute.
