Top Exit Points

From which pages do visitors commonly exit my site? This report shows the number of exits and number of pageviews for the top exit pages on your site.

Click the button on the far left of any entry to see metrics displayed over time.

To compare two date ranges, select the "Compare to Past" option on the Calendar tab.

Exits is the total number of times the page was an exit page. Pageviews is the total number of times the page was viewed. %Exit is Exits divided by Pageviews, expressed as a percentage.
