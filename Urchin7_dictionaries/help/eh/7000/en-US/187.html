CPC Program Analysis

For paid search, which search engine-keyword combinations drive the highest quality traffic? Specifically designed for cost-per-click search programs, this report shows the number of impressions (as provided by the search vendors), clicks, number of transactions, cost (from search ad vendor data and cost data you have supplied), Revenue, click-through rate, conversion rate, cost per click, revenue per click, and ROI ([Revenue - Cost]/Cost) for organic and paid search.

Click the plus next to any entry to drill down and compare keywords within each search engine.
Click the button at the far left of any entry to view the data over time or to cross-segment this data.

To compare two date ranges, select the "Compare to Past" option on the Calendar tab.
