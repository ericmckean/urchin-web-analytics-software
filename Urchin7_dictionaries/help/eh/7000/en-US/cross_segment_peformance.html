Cross Segment Peformance

This report cross references the primary segment in your main report with a secondary segment. Your primary segment is displayed in the tabs at the top left center of this report. The number of visits, pages per visit, conversion rates for each goal, and the average per visit value are shown.

  • To return to the main report, click the left-most tab (at the top left center of this report).
  • To change your secondary segment, select a segment from the pull down Segment menu at the top of this report.
