Organizations

Which companies and ISP connections account for the most revenue? This report aggregates revenue according to the companies and ISPs that host your visitors.

Click the button at the far left of any entry to view data over time.

To compare two date ranges, select the "Compare to Past" option on the Calendar tab.

Revenue is the total revenue resulting from e-commerce transactions by visitors from a specific organization. Trans is the number of transactions. $/Trans is Revenue divided by Transactions. Percent $ is the percentage of your site's total e-commerce revenue that visitors from the specific organization contribute.
