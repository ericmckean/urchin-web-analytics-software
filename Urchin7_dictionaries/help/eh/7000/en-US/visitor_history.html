Visitor History

This report provides an overview of information and a list of visits for the visitor you have selected. The overview in the top section of the report summarizes visits, conversions, and transactions, and provides location, referral, and platform information. The lower sections of the report display a list of visits and the clickpath for a selected visit. Click on a visit to view visit details, including the clickpath, displayed in the lowermost section of this report.

  • To return to a more general report, click a tab on the upper left of this report.
