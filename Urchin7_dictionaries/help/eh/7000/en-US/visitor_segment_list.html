Visitor Segment List

This report is a drill down of all the visitors that make up a specific line item on a report. The group of visitors to which these visitors belong is displayed in the tabs at the top left center of this report. The number of visits, pages per visit, conversion rates for each goal, and the average per visit value are shown for each visitor. Click on a visitor ID to drill down and view visitor identity and attributes, and to view a list of visits by date.

  • To return to the main report, click the tab (top left center of this report).
