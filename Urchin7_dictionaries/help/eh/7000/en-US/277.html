Product Categories

How much of each product category, product, and SKU do I sell? For e-commerce sites, understanding which products are selling on-line is crucial for generating relevant content, promotions and advertisements. This report shows the number of items sold, the total revenue, the average price, and the average order quantity for each product you sell online.

Click the plus next to any entry to drill down and view products. Drill down again to view SKUs.
Click the button at the far left of any entry to view data over time.
To compare two date ranges, select the "Compare to Past" option on the Calendar tab.
