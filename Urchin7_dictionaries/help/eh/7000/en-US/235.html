Day Parts Breakdown

At which hours of the day is each keyword most effective? This report compares the number of responses, and the goals or transactions per response for each keyword.

Click the plus next to any keyword to drill down and view these metrics by time of day.
Click the button at the far left of any entry to view the data over time.

To compare two date ranges, select the "Compare to Past" option on the Calendar tab.

Responses is the number of clicks that resulted in a visit to your site. G/R is Goals divided by Responses. T/R is Transactions divided by Responses.
