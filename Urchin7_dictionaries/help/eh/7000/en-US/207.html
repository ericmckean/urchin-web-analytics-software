Entrance Bounce Rates

Do visitors continue their visit after viewing their first page or do they immediately leave my site (Bounces)? This report lists the top entrance pages on which visitors land and their respective number of Bounces and Bounce Rates. A Bounce is counted each time a visitor immediately exits the site from the entrance page.

Click the button on the far left of any entry to see metrics displayed over time.

To compare two date ranges, select the "Compare to Past" option on the Calendar tab.
