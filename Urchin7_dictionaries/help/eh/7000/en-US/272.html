Affiliations

This report allows you to track the effectiveness of affiliate marketing. Marketers can use this report for commissioning or referral fees back to their affiliate channels. To populate this report, provide an affiliation in the transaction line of your e-commerce transactions (as explained in -->Capturing E-commerce Transactions<--).

Click the button at the far left of any entry to view data over time.

To compare two date ranges, select the "Compare to Past" option on the Calendar tab.

Revenue is the total revenue resulting from e-commerce transactions by visitors referred from each affiliate. Trans is the number of transactions. $/Trans is Revenue divided by Transactions. Percent $ is the percentage of your site's total e-commerce revenue that visitors referred by each affiliate contribute.
