New vs Returning

How do new visitors compare to returning visitors with respect to number of visits, number of transactions, total revenue, average visit value, and average transaction value?

Click the button at the far left of any entry to view data over time.

To compare two date ranges, select the "Compare to Past" option on the Calendar tab.

Visits is the total number of visits from new or returning visitors. Trans is the number of e-commerce transactions. Revenue is the total revenue from e-commerce transactions. $/Visits is Revenue divided by Visits. $/Trans is Revenue divided by Transactions.
